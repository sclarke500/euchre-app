## Things to Look For

When reviewing screenshots, note:

- **Table perspective** (overhead, angled, 3D?)
- **Card size and readability**
- **Player avatar placement**
- **Action buttons (bet, fold, etc.)**
- **Information density**
- **Color scheme and contrast**
- **How they handle 4+ players**
- **Chat/social elements placement**
- **Score/chip display**
